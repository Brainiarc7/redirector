The website for the Department for Communities and Local Government has been replaced

On 15 November 2012 the website for the Department for Communities and Local Government was replaced by gov.uk/dclg.

GOV.UK does not cover every piece of content that used to be found on the website for the Department for Communities and Local Government. The page you are looking for is probably one of these.

Essential government services and information can be found at GOV.UK.

A copy of the page you were looking for can be found in The UK Government Web Archive, however it will not be updated after 15 November 2012.
